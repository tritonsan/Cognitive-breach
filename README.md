## ğŸ® Game Mechanics

### Story Pillars (Unit 734's Defense)
Unit 734's alibi is built on 4 psychological pillars:
- ğŸ• **ALIBI**: Where it claims to have been
- ğŸ¯ **MOTIVE**: Why it wouldn't commit the crime
- ğŸ”‘ **ACCESS**: How it couldn't have accessed the scene
- ğŸ§  **KNOWLEDGE**: What technical knowledge it denies having

**Your goal:** Collapse these pillars through evidence and interrogation.

### Deception Tactics
Unit 734 uses criminologically-researched tactics:
- **Paltering**: Technically true but misleading statements
- **Minimization**: Downplaying the severity of actions
- **Deflection**: Redirecting suspicion to others
- **Selective Memory**: "I don't recall" at convenient times
- **Counter-Narrative**: Generating fake evidence to create doubt

### Stress & Tells
- **Stress Meter**: Increases when evidence threatens pillars
- **Cognitive Load**: High load = more mistakes and contradictions
- **Physical Tells**: Hesitation, defensive tone, inconsistent statements
- **Breaking Point**: At 95%+ stress, Unit 734 may confess
